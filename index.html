Slug Wars

Slug Wars is an online multiplayer 3rd-person shooter, where you play as cute Slugs, and after being eliminated you get to choose a power up! Slug Wars has been designed to be more approachable entry to the shooting game genre, and for the best experience we recommend playing with your friends!

Check it out on Steam!
